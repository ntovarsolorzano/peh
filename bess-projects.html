About the project

Premium Energy Holdings, LLC is the lead sponsor and developer of the Beacon Long Duration Energy Storage (BLDES) Project, developed together with Power-Tech Engineers, Inc. and Sumitomo Electric. The project deploys vanadium redox flow battery (VRFB) technology — a long-duration storage solution whose energy capacity can be scaled independently of power, with minimal degradation over decades of daily cycling.

The initial phase is sized at 50 MW with 5 hours of storage, proposed to LADWP and SCPPA as part of their long-duration energy storage procurement.
