About us

A developer of large energy storage resources, specialized in pumped storage systems.

Premium Energy Holdings, LLC was created in 2010. In affiliation with Power-Tech Engineers, Inc. (PTEI), we have worked on projects across the west and east coasts of the U.S. This alliance allows our customers to deploy their own and third-party Pumped Storage Project (PSP) and Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) developments — including feasibility studies, permitting, design, and construction.

The project portfolio

Pumped storage projects on both U.S. coasts and a growing long-duration battery portfolio — each designed to store and dispatch renewable energy at grid scale.

West coast · FERC P-14993 2,000 MW / 12 hr

Intermountain Pumped Storage

Millard County, Utah. A closed-loop plant using a new DMAD 2 reservoir as the lower pool and a new reservoir in the Canyon Mountains Range as the upper pool, interconnecting with the existing Intermountain AC Switchyard.
FERC P-15307 2,000 MW / 12 hr

Haiwee Pumped Storage

Inyo County, California. A new North Haiwee Reservoir 2 that also improves seismic reliability of the LA Aqueduct system.
FERC P-15002 2,000 MW / 12 hr

Walker Lake

Mineral County, Nevada. Walker Lake as the lower pool with a new upper reservoir in the Wassuk Range, interconnecting to the PDCI.
FERC P-15291 2,000 MW / 12 hr

Pyramid Lake

Washoe County, Nevada. Up to 2,900 ft of hydraulic head, paired with a proposed 2,000 MW solar PV farm.
FERC P-15035 2,000 MW / 12 hr

Isabella Lake

Kern County, California. Existing Isabella reservoir as the lower pool with a new upper reservoir in the Southern Sierra Nevada.
East coast · Upstate NY 700 MW / 12 hr

Ashokan PSP

Uses the Ashokan reservoir with a proposed upper reservoir of 226 acres, interconnecting to the existing Hurley Av. Substation via 230 kV lines.
East coast · Long Island, NY 500 MW / 6 hr

Shoreham PSP

A proposed 500 MW, 6-hour pumped storage resource supporting renewable integration on the east coast grid.
Battery storage · VRFB 50 MW / 5 hr initial

Beacon LDES

Our flagship long-duration battery project, developed with Sumitomo Electric using vanadium redox flow battery technology for LADWP and SCPPA.
