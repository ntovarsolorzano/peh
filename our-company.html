Our mission

Recognizing the future needs for cleaner, sustainable, and more efficient energy sources.

Premium Energy Holdings, LLC is an energy development company that recognizes the future needs and interests for cleaner, sustainable, and more efficient energy sources. Premium provides experienced technical services and develops projects that achieve the long-term goals of communities, local utilities, and large regions in their efforts to reduce the use of fossil fuels, produce and store energy, deliver power, and meet regulatory and reliability requirements.

In the development and delivery of these projects, Premium incorporates community interests, grid reliability, financial accountability, safety, and environmental stewardship — with a desire to develop long-term and lasting relationships and a model for future projects.

History

Born in 2010, built on three decades of utility experience.

2010 — Origins

Assisting developers and utilities across the USA

Our company was born in 2010, assisting solar, wind, hydro, and biomass power plant private developers and utilities all over the USA. Our key staff each carry more than 30 years of professional experience building and operating multi-billion dollar energy projects for water and power utilities.

Affiliation — Domestic

Power-Tech Engineers, Inc.

Through our affiliated USA engineering company — with over 25 years providing consulting, design, project and construction management services — we assisted in the development of green-field sustainable projects.

Affiliation — International

Power-Tech Engineers, S.A.

With our affiliated international company, we provided overseas Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) services to solar, battery storage, energy-efficient lighting (LED), environmental remediation, co-generation and other energy projects for the oil, water and power industries.

Pioneers

Early adopters of battery energy storage

We were pioneers in the application of battery energy storage systems as a solution for optimum power system reliability. Today we are the lead sponsor and developer of the Beacon Long Duration Energy Storage Project, focused on long-duration energy storage for SCPPA and LADWP.
